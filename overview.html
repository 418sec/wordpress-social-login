Plugin Overview

WordPress Social Login is a Charityware.

If you download any use the plug-in on a live website you are encouraged to donate to any charity organization of your choosing.
Below is a list of organizations and causes we support.

What is WSL?

  • Social authentication. One click login,
  • Collect accurate users profiles, import users friends list,
  • Absurdly easy to install, use and to integrate *
  • Open source, Free, unlimited, white-label Careware.

What is not?

  • Social sharing buttons, for this there is that,
  • Some nice looking social widget to add for aesthetic purposes,
  • A plugin that rely on an external middleman service,
  • Open core, feature limited Crippleware.

Key Features

  • Absolute privacy of your website users data.
  • Wide variety of identities providers (25+ IDP).
  • A highly customizable and fully extensible widgets.
  • Easy-to-use and clean user interfaces.
  • Contacts import from Google, Facebook, Live and Linkedin.
  • User profiles and contacts management.
  • Compatible with WordPress 3.0+, BuddyPress and bbPress.
  • ACL-based security model.
  • Provides a direct access to social networks apis.
  • Modular design easily extended.
  • Comprehensive documentation.

Requirements

  • PHP >= 5.2.0 installed,
  • WSL Endpoint URLs reachable,
  • PHP/Session enabled and working,
  • PHP/CURL extension with SSL enabled,
  • REGISTER_GLOBALS are Off,

* Since WordPress Social Login goal is to provide an alternative to all the SaaS and third-parties services by bringing the whole "thing" into your website, the installation could turn out to be a real pain in the ass on some rare cases. — A random intellectual honesty moment.
