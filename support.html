Contact & Support




Community Support   Github issues tracker   Contact Developer




  1. Community Support: WordPress has an amazing community where people help each other and learn together. You may leave your support request there but keep in mind that it's a community driven, low activity forum and therefore your request may not be answered on timely fashion.
  2. Github issues tracker: This method is meant for collaboration. You may use it to either report a bug or to request a feature, and if you'd submit code, that woud be even better. Please avoid posting one-line issues on Github.
  3. Contact Developer: Contact email address for business inquiries. If you just want to say hi, that's cool too.
